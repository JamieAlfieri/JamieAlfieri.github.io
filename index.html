Home

Howdy! My name is Jamie Alfieri, and I am currently a PhD Candidate in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at Texas A&M University. I research population, quantitative, and functional genomics of wild and domesticated species. I am coadvised by Giri Athrey and Heath Blackmon.
